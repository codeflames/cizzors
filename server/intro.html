Cizzors

A simple and powerful URL shortening service

Key Features

URL Shortening

Shorten long URLs with just a click, making them easier to share and remember.

Custom URLs

Create branded short links with custom domain names to reinforce your brand identity.

QR Code Generation

Generate QR codes for your shortened URLs to enhance offline marketing and engagement.

Analytics

Track link performance with basic analytics and gain insights into click-through rates.

Link History

Access and manage your previously created links easily for efficient link management.

API Documentation
